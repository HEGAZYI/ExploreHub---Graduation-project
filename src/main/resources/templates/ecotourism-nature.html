Egypt's Natural Heritage Conservation

Egypt is committed to protecting its extraordinary natural heritage through innovative ecotourism initiatives that balance conservation with sustainable development. From the pristine coral reefs of the Red Sea to the unique desert ecosystems of the Western Desert, Egypt's protected areas showcase the country's biodiversity while providing economic opportunities for local communities. These initiatives ensure that Egypt's natural wonders will be preserved for future generations to enjoy.

Sacred Oasis

Siwa Oasis Protected Area

Egypt's most remote and mystical oasis, home to the legendary Oracle of Amun and unique Berber culture. This protected area preserves traditional architecture, salt lakes, and ancient customs that have remained unchanged for thousands of years. The oasis is a living museum of desert traditions and sustainable practices.
