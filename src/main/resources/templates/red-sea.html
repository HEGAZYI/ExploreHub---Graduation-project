Egypt's Marine Paradise

The Red Sea is a world-renowned destination for marine enthusiasts, offering crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and year-round sunshine. Stretching along Egypt's eastern coast, this aquatic paradise features world-class diving sites, luxurious resorts, and endless opportunities for water sports. From the bustling nightlife of Hurghada to the pristine beaches of Marsa Alam, the Red Sea caters to every type of traveler seeking sun, sea, and adventure.

Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

March to June & September to November - Ideal temperatures (25-30¬∞C), calm waters, perfect for diving and water sports. Peak season with great weather conditions.

July to August - Hottest months (35-40¬∞C), but fewer crowds and lower prices. Early morning activities recommended.

December to February - Cooler temperatures (20-25¬∞C), pleasant for all activities. Some windier days good for water sports.

Diving & Water Safety

Certification: Choose PADI/SSI certified dive centers. Check instructor qualifications and group sizes.

Equipment: Bring your own gear or rent from reputable centers. Check equipment before each dive.

Marine Protection: Use reef-safe sunscreen, don't touch coral, maintain safe distance from marine life.

Health: Stay hydrated, avoid diving within 24 hours of flying, know your limits.

What to Pack

Beach Wear: Swimwear, cover-ups, sun hat, sunglasses, reef-safe sunscreen.

Water Sports: Rash guard, water shoes, waterproof camera, action camera.

Desert Tours: Light layers, closed shoes, scarf for sand protection, warm jacket for evenings.

Essentials: First aid kit, insect repellent, reusable water bottle, power bank.
