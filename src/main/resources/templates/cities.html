Discover Egypt's Magnificent Cities

Egypt's cities are a mesmerizing blend of ancient wonders and modern life. From the bustling metropolis of Cairo with its iconic pyramids and vibrant markets, to the serene beauty of Aswan's Nile islands, each city tells a unique story spanning thousands of years. Explore the Mediterranean charm of Alexandria, dive into the Red Sea paradise of Sharm El-Sheikh, or wander through the open-air museum of Luxor where pharaohs built their eternal monuments.
